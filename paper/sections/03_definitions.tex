\section{Definitions}
An AXZ audit complex is a diagram
\[
C \xrightarrow{\partial_1} R \xrightarrow{\partial_2} G \xrightarrow{\partial_3} H
\]
of finite-dimensional vector spaces over $\mathbb F_2$ satisfying $\partial_2\partial_1=0$ and $\partial_3\partial_2=0$. Define $H_R^{AXZ}=\ker(\partial_2)/\operatorname{im}(\partial_1)$ and $H_G^{AXZ}=\ker(\partial_3)/\operatorname{im}(\partial_2)$.
